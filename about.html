About MCSLang

Our Mission

MCSLang is a powerful scripting language designed specifically for Minecraft 1.21.X. Our goal is to provide developers with an efficient and intuitive way to create complex Minecraft Builds.

Key Features

• Simple and intuitive syntax
• Built-in Minecraft-specific functions
• High performance and reliability
• Extensive documentation and examples
